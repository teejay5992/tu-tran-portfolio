About Me




Welcome to my creative space. My name is Tu Tran, and I am also known as Tee Jay. I'm a young, passionate visual designer and illustrator who wants to make the world a beautiful place. I want to inspire other designers with my skills and diligence. You may still wonder who I am to expect. Don't worry, let me ride you back in time and figure out!

When I was a little kid, I always grabbed a pencil to sketch my favorite comic characters and taped all my drawings on my wall like an art exhibition.
