Glitch Art


The photos portrayed how I was alone and used technology (tablet and phone) as the only way to communicate with the world. The repetition elements in the glitched photos created a sense of dynamic movement in the still photos, which reminds me of Sabasto Visconti's work. To me, These glitched photos represent a relationship between human, technology, art, and time (of the pandemic).
