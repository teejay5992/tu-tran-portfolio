Prosthetic Modeling


I want to address the modern lifestyle in which most people are so involved with the internet and technology, and my Cloudometer is a response to that topic. Cloudometer is a set of prostheses that allows the user to measure and track their data usage from the technology around them. The AR headset can scan the environment to look for devices used by the user. It analyzes and transfers the collected data to a built-in computer inside the suit for storage. The data is then transmitted to an expandable cloud to manifest intangible data into physical form. For the user’s convenience, the cloud’s wire is connected to the suit through a connector that is mobilized around the belt. Cloudometer is both a technology and an art form that portrays how humans nowadays are always connected to the digital cloud.
